Privacy and Cookies

Privacy Summary

None of our products record or sell any personal identifying information. Some of our projects store personal information on-device for personalization purposes only. The only things sent to our backends are anonymous data like analytics and user-inputted usernames and configuration.

Discord Bots

Our Discord bots store server names and their config files. They also store messages flagged by meese detection along with that message author's user ID for debug purposes only. No other user information or activity is stored, and what is stored is accessible to the JCWYT team only. If you have any questions about data storage, email us at info@jcwyt.com.

Leaderboards

Our leaderboards are stored through LootLocker. Anything you enter as your name can and most likely will be displayed on the public leaderboard. We do our best to censor offensive language. That being said, it is all user-added data and we don't take responsibity for any harm leaderboard names may cause. If you see a name and want to report it, let us know at bugs@jcwyt.com.

Analytics

We take analytic data on most of our websites through Google Analytics, which includes city and device information. That data is not tied to your IP or name.
